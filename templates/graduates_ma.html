Career Options for M.A Graduates

Arts and Humanities Careers

As an M.A graduate, you have a wide range of career options in various fields. Here are some popular career paths:

College Professor

Teach undergraduate and graduate students in universities and colleges. Conduct research, publish papers, and mentor students in your field of expertise.

Content Writer/Editor

Create and edit content for various platforms including websites, magazines, books, and academic publications. Develop engaging and informative content.

Research Analyst

Conduct research in your specialized field, analyze data, and provide insights. Work with think tanks, research institutions, or government agencies.

Public Relations Specialist

Manage communication between organizations and the public. Create and maintain a positive public image for individuals, groups, or organizations.

Archivist/Curator

Preserve and organize important historical documents, artifacts, and records. Work in museums, libraries, or historical societies.

Social Worker

Help individuals, families, and communities enhance their well-being. Address social problems and advocate for vulnerable populations.

Higher Education Options

After completing your M.A, you might consider pursuing higher education:

  • Ph.D in your specialized field
  • Post-graduate diplomas in specialized areas
  • Professional certifications in teaching, counseling, etc.
  • Second master's degree in a complementary field
